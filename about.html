About Me

Here are some passions of mine

Read More
Weightlifting

Although I love all forms of physical activity, nothing compares to weightlifting. Due to the pandemic, working out at the gym became increasingly difficult, so I invested into a gym setup in my basement. There, I have a bench, bar, squat rack, weights and dumbbells. My favourite exercise is the bench press and my current personal record is 255 pounds.

Clarinet

I've been playing the clarinet since the eighth grade. In high school, I was a part of the school's band where we had the privilege to perform at Carnegie Hall in New York. Although I don't play in a band currently, I will forever remember the feeling of stepping on the stage and performing after putting in hours of practice for weeks on end.

Cooking

Food and cooking has always been a big part of my life. My grandparents used to own a Chinese restaurant while my uncle owned a Chinese barbeque place where I worked during the summers cutting meat and running the cash register. Although I am definitely not as good of a cook as any of them, I still take pride in my cooking. I have recently started learning Korean cuisine making dishes such as jjajangmyeon and gamjatang.

Gaming

Growing up, I was always surrounded by games. Although not very age appropriate, I would watch my dad play games like God of War and Resident Evil 4. Nowadays, I like playing online computer games such as League of Legends and Valorant with my friends.
